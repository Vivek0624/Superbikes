Superbikes Origin

Launched in 1969, it was the Japanese manufacturer’s first attempt at building a bike for the American market, It’s nearly 50 years since the modern superbike was created. These are bikes optimised for road racing, with engines of 1000cc capacity, or thereabouts.

SuperBikes categories

Electric

E-bikes use rechargeable batteries that can travel up to 25 to 45 km/h, much faster than most people would cycle.

Sport

Sport bikes emphasize top speed, acceleration, braking, handling and grip on paved roads.

Standard

Standards, also called naked bikes or roadsters, are versatile, general-purpose street motorcycles.
